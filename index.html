Bean stew with rice

Ingredients
    • Preparation
    We put the beans to soak from the night before. In a high pot (or in the pressure cooker) with cold water we put the beans with the head of garlic (with a knife cut around it), the chorizo in a piece, the chopped dewlap and the bay leaf. We cover them just with water and when the boiling starts we cut it a couple of times adding cold water. We let them cook until they are tender (between 45 min and 1 hour at low heat. In pressure cooker it depends on the pots).
    In the meantime we prepare a sofrito with the chopped onion and the carrot and saut√©ed on the fire, add the tomato, the green beans and the paprika. Salt and let the tomato reduce a little. It is not necessary to bury the vegetables because they will cook with the stew.
    Add this sauce to the stew when the beans are already tender along with the rice and let it cook for about 15 minutes, stirring the pan back and forth so that the beans do not fall apart and the sauce gets a little thicker. Remove the chorizo, cut it into slices and put it back into the stew. Serve the bean stew with hot rice after 10 minutes of rest.
    Cook the beans with the chorizo, the dewlap and the bay leaf in the pot and program the stew menu, 20 min, max. pressure. Depressurize and add the sofrito. Stir and close the pot, stew menu, 5 min. medium pressure. And serve the bean stew with rice hot.
